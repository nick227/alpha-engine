## What it is
Alpha Engine is a pipeline that transforms news/media + market context into **structured events**, **strategy predictions**, and **measured outcomes** that can be analyzed and iterated on.

## Who it’s for
- Research teams and analysts who need repeatable signal evaluation
- Builders who want an extensible ingestion + prediction stack
- Evaluators who want transparent measurement (confidence vs outcomes)

## What you get (outputs)
- Structured event scoring
- Predictions with horizons/confidence
- Outcome evaluation and strategy ranking
- Dashboards for analysis and auditability

Concrete evidence artifacts in this repo:
- Demo CSV exports: `outputs/` (generated by `python scripts/demo_run.py`)
- Backfill/replay DB: `data/alpha.db` (tables include `predictions` + `prediction_outcomes`)

## Why now
- Market-moving information is unstructured and high-volume.
- Research teams need reproducible pipelines, not one-off notebooks.
- “AI predictions” are only valuable when paired with measurement and failure-mode transparency.

## Differentiators
- Closed-loop measurement: predictions → outcomes → ranking/learning
- Extensible ingestion: declarative adapters and source config
- Clear separation: semantic APIs, canonical chart shapes, consistent outputs
- Audit-friendly artifacts: database persistence and exportable `outputs/`

## Roadmap positioning
- Tier 1 offer: **research & analytics** (evaluation, ranking, explainability)
- Advanced/future goal: automated trading/execution (handled as a roadmap milestone, not a current claim)

## How to verify this in the repo (evidence-based)
- Fast demo artifacts (no network): `python scripts/demo_run.py` → `outputs/`
- Auditable DB trail (history): `python -m app.ingest.backfill_cli backfill-range --start YYYY-MM-DD --end YYYY-MM-DD` → `data/alpha.db`
- UI inspection: `python -m streamlit run app/ui/app.py` (set `ALPHA_DB_PATH` if needed)
